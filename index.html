Meet Kirby

He's pink. He's round. And he's puffy. He's been on countless adventures, and he's saved his planet, Pop Star, tons of times, too. He can inhale enemies and gain their powers. He's been around for over thirty years and is loved by all. His games are so sweet that he'll give you a sugar rush. And he's so cute that you can't help but smile.
